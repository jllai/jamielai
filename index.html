About Me


Let's get in touch!

Cello there! My name's Jamie Lai and I'm currently a senior at the Univeristy of Michigan studying UX Design at the School of Information. I chose UX design as my life path because it encapsulates everything I'm interested in: technology, human psychology and behavior, and aesthetic beauty. UX Design democratizes technology and makes everything more accessible, and I want to contribute to that ideal. I'm currently looking for a full-time position as a UX/UI Designer.

Beyond UX Design, I have a passion in social and environmental justice, traveling, and cooking. When I'm not out hiking or rock climbing, I'm inside reading, watching video essays, and playing with my adorable kitten, Haku!
